About Myself

I am a senior studying computer science, looking to complete my B.Tech degree. I am mainly interested in the magic that happens underneath any complex system. I love backend engineering and systems engineering. I try to create everyday things to better understand them. I firmly believe Feynman's words - "What I cannot create, I do not understand". Few years back, I used to think Git was magic. But ever since I created my own version control system in python, I have been at peace. Currently I am implementing Redis and a KV pair basic database.

I also watch Formula One in my free time, mostly supporting Ferrari and Red Bull. I essentially want to become a polymath as my interest lie over a variety of domains and fields.
